Shengjie Xu

I am a second-year Computer Science Ph.D. student at the University of Maryland, advised by Professor Ming C. Lin. I received my M.S. in ECE from UC Santa Cruz (co-advised by Prof. Leilani H. Gilpin and Professor James E. Davis) and my B.S. from Hebei University of Technology.

From 2011 to 2021, I worked at CATARC, developing computer vision and control systems for autonomous vehicles.
